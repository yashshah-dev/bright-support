## 10. Risk Mitigation & Contingencies

### Potential Challenges & Solutions

#### **Challenge 1: Low Initial Conversion Rates**
**Risk:** Landing pages don't convert as expected  
**Mitigation:**
- Aggressive A/B testing (weekly iterations)
- User testing with real NDIS participants
- Simplify forms, add live chat
- Offer stronger incentives (free first assessment)

#### **Challenge 2: High Cost Per Lead**
**Risk:** Paid ads exceed $50 CPA  
**Mitigation:**
- Pause underperforming campaigns immediately
- Focus on organic channels (SEO, referrals)
- Negotiate better ad placements
- Refine targeting (exclude non-NDIS audiences)

#### **Challenge 3: Content Production Bottleneck**
**Risk:** Can't maintain 2 posts/week schedule  
**Mitigation:**
- Hire 1-2 freelance writers
- Repurpose content (blog ‚Üí video ‚Üí social)
- Batch content creation (4 posts at once)
- User-generated content campaigns

#### **Challenge 4: Partnership Rejections**
**Risk:** GPs/OTs don't want to partner  
**Mitigation:**
- Offer free NDIS training for their staff
- Provide co-branded marketing materials
- Focus on non-compete partnerships (complementary services)
- Build relationships over time (long-term play)

#### **Challenge 5: Algorithm Changes**
**Risk:** Google/Facebook algorithm updates hurt performance  
**Mitigation:**
- Diversify traffic sources (don't rely on one channel)
- Focus on owned channels (email list, YouTube subscribers)
- Stay updated on industry news
- Build strong brand (direct traffic immunity)
